WORDSPOTTING DEMO FOR INDIAN LANGUAGES




This application is built to demonstrate handwritten document retrieval systems for collections written in different Indic scripts. Handwritten documents written by a specific person or handwritten documents containing information related to a specific domain that may hold a historical significance are referred to as Collections in this project.
To the best of our knowledge, this application is the first attempt to build a retreival system for handwritten documents written in Indian languages.



Instructions

The demonstration systems have been built for various collections. These collections are displayed in the right panel of the page. Querying and document retireval is performed collection wise. To use the system and to learn more about different collections, click on any of the collections.
